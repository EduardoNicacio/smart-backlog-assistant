# Email Router Service – Product Features

---

## Feature 1: Multi-Provider Mailbox Connection

**Feature Name:** Multi-Provider Mailbox Connection

**Description:**
Enables IT Administrators to connect one or more mailboxes from any supported email provider — including IMAP/SMTP, Microsoft 365, and Google Workspace — so that the routing service can receive and process incoming emails regardless of the organisation's existing email infrastructure.

**Key Functionality:**

- IMAP/SMTP mailbox connection for generic email providers
- Microsoft 365 OAuth-based mailbox connection
- Google Workspace OAuth-based mailbox connection
- Support for connecting multiple mailboxes simultaneously

**User Benefit:**
IT Administrators can onboard the routing service against their existing email environment without migration or infrastructure changes, reducing deployment friction and time to value.

---

## Feature 2: Flexible Email Ingestion

**Feature Name:** Flexible Email Ingestion

**Description:**
Controls how and when emails enter the routing service, offering both real-time and scheduled polling ingestion modes, with full support for emails carrying large attachments, ensuring no message content is lost or delayed beyond operational requirements.

**Key Functionality:**

- Real-time ingestion for immediate routing decisions
- Configurable polling schedules for controlled mailbox checks
- Ingestion of emails with large attachments without content loss

**User Benefit:**
IT Administrators can align ingestion behaviour with operational and infrastructure requirements, while Customer Support Agents receive complete email content — including attachments — ensuring nothing is missed.

---

## Feature 3: Automated Email Classification

**Feature Name:** Automated Email Classification

**Description:**
Applies Natural Language Processing to analyse and categorise incoming emails automatically, supporting multi-label classification, language detection, and confidence scoring so that emails are accurately sorted and directed without manual intervention.

**Key Functionality:**

- NLP-based analysis and category assignment
- Assignment of one or more category labels per email
- Automatic detection of email language for language-based routing
- Confidence scoring with flagging of low-confidence results for human review

**User Benefit:**
Customer Support Agents spend less time manually triaging emails, while Team Leads gain confidence that complex or ambiguous emails are handled accurately and reviewed where necessary.

---

## Feature 4: Routing Rules Engine

**Feature Name:** Routing Rules Engine

**Description:**
Provides IT Administrators with a no-code interface to define, prioritise, and manage routing rules based on a range of conditions — including sender domain, keywords, category label, time of day, and team availability — with fallback routes ensuring emails are always delivered.

**Key Functionality:**

- Rule definition using multiple condition types (sender domain, keywords, category, time of day, team availability)
- Priority ordering across multiple rules
- Fallback route configuration to handle unmatched conditions
- No-code rule builder UI for self-service configuration without developer involvement

**User Benefit:**
IT Administrators can configure and maintain sophisticated routing logic independently, reducing dependency on engineering teams and ensuring emails always reach the correct destination.

---

## Feature 5: SLA Management and Escalation

**Feature Name:** SLA Management and Escalation

**Description:**

**Key Functionality:**

- SLA target definition per email category
- Automatic escalation triggering when an SLA breach is imminent
- Escalation notifications delivered via email
- Escalation notifications delivered via Slack

**User Benefit:**
Team Leads can enforce consistent response time standards across the organisation and are proactively alerted through their preferred channels before a breach occurs, reducing SLA failures.

---

## Feature 6: Operational Analytics and Reporting

**Feature Name:** Operational Analytics and Reporting

- Historical reports on average response time
- Historical reports on escalation rates
- CSV export for external analysis
- PDF export for stakeholder reporting

Team Leads can monitor live operations, identify performance trends, allocate resources effectively, and communicate results to stakeholders — all from a single reporting capability.
