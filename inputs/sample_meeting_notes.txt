Meeting Notes - Product Discovery Session
Date: 2024-01-15
Attendees: Sarah (PM), Dev (Tech Lead), Priya (Designer), Marcus (Stakeholder)

---

AGENDA: New Customer Portal Requirements

Sarah opened the session by outlining the goal: replace the legacy customer-facing
portal before Q3. The current system has a 34% abandonment rate on the account
setup flow and support tickets regularly cite "can't find my invoices."

KEY DISCUSSION POINTS:

1. Authentication & Onboarding
   - Marcus wants SSO via Google and Microsoft for enterprise customers
   - Sarah mentioned that password reset emails take up to 10 minutes currently
     - this is a known pain point from NPS surveys
   - Dev flagged that we'll need MFA support to satisfy the upcoming SOC 2 audit
   - Priya shared research: 60% of new users drop off before completing profile setup.
     She wants to redesign the onboarding to a 3-step wizard with progress indicators.

2. Invoice & Billing
   - Top support request: customers can't find or download past invoices
   - Marcus wants customers to be able to self-serve billing changes (upgrade/downgrade plan)
   - Auto-pay setup is frequently broken on Safari - Dev to investigate
   - Requirement: invoices must be available as PDF downloads going back 24 months

3. Support & Help
   - Current process: email only, avg response 2.3 days
   - The team agreed we should integrate a live chat widget (Intercom was mentioned)
   - Sarah wants an AI-assisted FAQ - common questions should be auto-answered
   - Tickets should show status to the customer (open/in-progress/resolved)

4. Notifications
   - Customers want email + SMS notifications for payment failures
   - Dev noted the current system sends no notifications at all for failed payments
   - Priya: notification preferences should be configurable per-user
