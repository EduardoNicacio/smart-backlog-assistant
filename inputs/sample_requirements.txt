Product Specification: Email Router Service
===========================================

Overview
--------
The Email Router Service is a cloud-based platform that automatically classifies,
prioritizes, and routes incoming emails to the correct team or individual based on
content analysis, sender history, and configurable business rules.

The system is designed for mid-to-large organizations that receive high volumes of
customer and internal email across multiple inboxes.

Core Capabilities
-----------------
1. Email Ingestion
   - Connect to one or more mailboxes via IMAP/SMTP or Microsoft 365 / Google Workspace APIs.
   - Ingest emails in real time or on a polling schedule.
   - Support attachments up to 25 MB.

2. Classification Engine
   - analyze email subject, body, and metadata using NLP.
   - Assign one or more category labels (e.g. Billing, Support, Sales, HR).
   - Detect language and route accordingly.
   - Confidence score per classification; flag low-confidence emails for human review.

3. Routing Rules
   - Administrators can define rules based on: sender domain, keywords, category label, time of day, and team availability.
   - Rules support priority ordering and fallback routes.
   - No-code rule builder UI for non-technical administrators.

4. Escalation and SLA Management
   - Define SLA targets per category (e.g. Support emails answered within 4 hours).
   - Automatic escalation when SLA is at risk.
   - Notification to team leads via email or Slack when escalation is triggered.

5. Analytics Dashboard
   - Real-time view of inbox volume, routing decisions, and SLA status.
   - Historical reports: volume by category, average response time, escalation rate.
   - Export reports as CSV or PDF.

6. Integrations
   - CRM: Salesforce, HubSpot.
   - Ticketing: Jira Service Management, Zendesk.
   - Messaging: Slack, Microsoft Teams.

Non-Functional Requirements
---------------------------
- Availability: 99.9 % uptime SLA.
- Latency: emails routed within 5 seconds of ingestion.
- Security: SOC 2 Type II compliant; data encrypted at rest and in transit.
- Scalability: handle up to 50,000 emails per day per organization.

Users
-----
- Customer Support Agents   : receive and action routed emails.
- Sales Representatives     : receive routed sales enquiries.
- IT Administrators         : configure mailboxes, routing rules, and integrations.
- Team Leads / Managers     : monitor SLA, receive escalation alerts, view reports.
- End Customers             : send emails that are ingested by the system.
